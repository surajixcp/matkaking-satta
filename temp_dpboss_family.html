Matka Games: Different Variants

With the time and interest of players in Satta Matka games online and the online Matka app, the popularity of these games has reached its peak.

Single Patti: Players bet on a single digit that can be anything between 0-9. All matka games rely on these numbers for betting.

Jodi: Players must make two number pairs. As usual, the betting numbers are between 00 and 99. During the live matka results, if the number that has been drawn randomly is the same number they have placed for betting, they win and may become the online Satta king.

Half Sangam: It is known as a combination betting that combines one number picked from a set and combined with another number from another set.

Full Sangam: Two numbers are selected from the two sets and if it is the final number that has been drawn, the player wins.

Cycle patti: It involves betting on three numbers as per a format or “cycle”. Betting should be placed in a particular sequence. If the cycle opted for and selected by the player is the final chosen (or drawn) number, the player wins the game.
